## What is Pypeline?
Pypeline is the infrastructure company behind tool-agnostic platforms like **SimShell** and **Tencil**. 

We do not build end-user consumer applications. We build the "pipelines" that connect disconnected systems, verify ground truth, and create safe environments where humans and AI agents can collaborate without breaking production.

## The Name
The name **Pypeline** is a technical portmanteau:
1. **Pipeline:** The infrastructure that connects point A to point B. Whether it is a CI/CD pipeline for verifying code, or a data pipeline for translating files, pipelines are how structured work gets done.
2. **Py:** A nod to Python, the foundational language of the AI era. It signals our commitment to AI-human workflows.

## Our Products

### [Tencil](./tencil-dev/) — The Design Pipeline
Tencil provides a protocol for cross-domain design translation. It acts as the pipeline that takes data from one tool (like Penpot), standardizes it against a canonical format, and pushes it to another tool (like Pencil.dev). It ensures ground truth in design.

### [SimShell](./SimShell/) — The Skills Pipeline
SimShell is a flight simulator for CLI and AI agent training. It acts as the CI/CD pipeline for human and AI skills—allowing you to practice, make errors, and verify workflows *before* touching real production servers.
