# About Pypeline

Tencil is a core product of **Pypeline**.

## What is Pypeline?
Pypeline develops infrastructure that bridges tools, ensuring verifiable, safe AI-human workflows across domains. 

Where **SimShell** (our sister project) acts as the *skills and verification pipeline* for developers and AI agents, **Tencil** acts as the *design data pipeline*. Tencil is the underlying protocol that bridges disconnected tools, ensuring ground truth when moving complex structures between domains (e.g., UI, CAD, Medical).

To learn more about the Pypeline ecosystem and our mission to build tool-agnostic infrastructure, read the overarching [Pypeline Manifesto](../PYPELINE.md).
